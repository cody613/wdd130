Overview

Purpose

At H2OH! our goal is to increase the number whitewater rafters both visiting and living in Idaho. We believe that not enough people are aware of the joys of rafting, nor are they aware of the amazing places to do so within our wonderfully diverse state. To help facilitate all your rafting needs, we will provide unbeatable prices on both equipment and guides. We'll see you out on the water!

Audience

Do you ever feel like your life could use a little more excitment? Are you tired of the same old thing? The same old roads you drive everyday? Then try H20H!'s whitewater rafting at a river near you! Idaho boasts 3,100+ miles of whitewater river so you're likely to never see the same fish twice! And with our unbeatable prices you'll be on the water more than land in no time!
